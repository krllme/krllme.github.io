Welcome to be here!

Daniel Smith is a musician and visual artist living in Port Richmond, Philadelphia. He is the drummer for the band Low Dose and the guitarist and vocalist for the band Sleep Tactics

Daniel graduated from the University of the Arts with a BFA in Illustration in 2009, and has since created a catalog of illustrative design work in conjunction with his career in music, centered in album art, merchandise, show posters, and promotional materials
